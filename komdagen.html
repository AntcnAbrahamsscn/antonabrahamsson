Kommunikatörsdagen 2021

Kommunikatörsdagen is an yearly job fair for students and professionals at Karlstad University. I created a new graphic profile for the fair. I made it more modern with the aim to portray the importance of networking and communication in the leaves of a branch. My work also included to create all the graphic content for the fair's media platforms, website but also material for marketing in physical places.
