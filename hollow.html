Hollow Surf Apparels

HOLLOW is a fictional surf brand. I created a graphic profile and developed the campaign "Mind the waves". My goal was to make a surf brand that stands out in the market with a stylish and modern touch. This  through an edgy and futuristic style where the name and the logotype of the brand refers to a hollow wave.
